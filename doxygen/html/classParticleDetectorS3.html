Define an S3 detector (or subset thereof)
id - define the experiment index, for use in naming conventions of histograms
z - the offset in z of the detector from the target position
x_off - the offset (from the beam axis) of the S3 detector in the x-direction
y_off - the offset (from the beam axis) of the S3 detector in the y-direction
ring_in - the innermost ring of the S3 in the experimental range
ring_out - the outermost ring of the S3 in this experimental range
tmin & tmax - define the maximum and minimum theta values in the efficiency histograms, this must cover a larger range than the detector
