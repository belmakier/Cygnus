\subsection{Detailed Description}
Markov-\/\-Chain Monte Carlo iterator. 

\hyperlink{classMCMCWalker}{M\-C\-M\-C\-Walker}

Using a Metropolis-\/\-Hastings algorithm the \hyperlink{classMCMCWalker}{M\-C\-M\-C\-Walker} class iterates through the parameter space, with a weighting towards the most likely solutions, causing eventual convergence while sampling the probability distribution of the parameters.
